06

Six lessons we'd hand to nextbike

01
Understand the environment
León is older and price-sensitive — strategy must start from the city, not the product.
02
Target audience is key
A precise 18–40 beats broad awareness on a €500 budget, every time.
03
Maintain persona testing
Let Campaign-1 data decide between Lucía and Carlos — don't guess the winner.
04
Use real León photos
Local landmarks and real streets outperform generic bike-share imagery.
05
Plan for contingency
Delays happen — build response time into every campaign calendar.
06
Understand customers
Talk to real riders in León; position nextbike as a possibility, not a necessity.
